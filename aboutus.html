ABOUT

Welcome Home

The Wander Nest was born with the belief that home wasn’t just a place, but a “feeling”. A feeling that “traveling” and “home” were not opposing ideas, but complimentary ones that co-existed and enabled each other. A feeling that kept you safe, pampered and cared for.

We envisioned a destination home that inspires you to explore and look for life-changing experiences, ones that would create wonderful memories to be shared and treasured by friends and loved ones, for many years to come. To make that dream come to life, we crafted a place that would attract laid-back travelers in search for a home-away-from-home in Bohol, one that offered affordable luxury, nature and hospitality that is to be felt and remembered.

Our team invites you to come stay with us in our beautiful sanctuary. It is our hope and mantra that you will indeed Find Your Bliss with us.

We can’t wait to welcome you “home”.

Peaceful & Private

Decidedly situated away from the noisy hustle and bustle of crowded tourist areas, you'll find our boutique hideaway nestled within quiet forest surroundings. With our complimentary daily shuttles to nearby beaches and restaurants, you'll feel like you're living in your own private resort with your own chauffered vehicles.

High Class Comfort

Set inside a secure 1,100sqm private compound, you'll feel safe and right at home with the soothing comforts of minimalist design, high-quality interiors, and lush, meticulously landscaped gardens.

We pride ourselves by having a well-appointed villa and poolside cabana with resort-like amenities. Our property boasts a large pool, kids pool, jacuzzi, fully-wood pool deck, furnished with cozy beanbag chairs, teak wood lounge chairs, modern concrete tables, parasols, a bar reception area, al fresco dining patio and a bonfire garden chill out area - all carefully planned and crafted with our guests' comfort and enjoyment in mind.

Your Vacation Home

Our property has 6 areas for guests to stay in, all with their own fully-equipped modern kitchens, private dining, living spaces and high-spec audio-visual equipment.

Each can be booked separately by individual groups (2-8pax) or as a whole, for an all-exclusive stay. Perfect for large groups of friends or families (up to 25pax).

All 9 bedrooms are set with 5 star hotel-standard linen, towels and memory foam mattresses for a guaranteed comfortable night's rest.

Thoughtfully Designed Spaces

Our unique approach to space – a balance between private and shared – means you’re free to choose what suits a moment’s mood. You’ll always have a cozy cocoon-like room or one of our vibrant communal areas to return to throughout the day.

Hospitable & Attentive Staff

We have well-trained staff who take pride in caring for our guests' needs at all times. They are there to make your stay as enjoyable as possible and will closely coordinate with you to ensure that you have the most comfortable, relaxing and memorable stay with us.
