Find Your Bliss at THE WANDER NEST

Find Your Bliss at THE WANDER NEST

The Wander Nest is a new, serviced, luxury bed and breakfast, located on the beautiful island of Panglao, Bohol, Philippines. Your tropical destination home and private gateway to exciting Bohol adventures, inspired by nature and local culture.

With the amenities of a private resort, modern comforts of your own vacation home and our on-site staff to take care of all your needs - friends, families and couples will surely savor everything that our property and Bohol has to offer.

Location

With our quiet, all-natural surroundings, we provide a peaceful respite for kindred spirits in search for exclusivity and privacy. We offer complimentary airport pick up and daily beach shuttles, so you’re also just a few minutes away from Panglao’s tourist attractions.

Services & Facilities

Accomodations

Our compound houses 6 accommodation options, varying in size and occupancy. For large groups we have 3 villas, and for smaller groups 1 poolside Cabana with 3 separate rooms. With this unique offering, mixed groups may choose and combine different areas to match their needs.

The 3 villas are best suited for large groups; the Main Villa (up to 8pax), Garden Villa (up to 7pax) and Sunset Villa (4pax). These villas can be conjoined by connecting doors to be shared by more people, or can be booked separately for a more private and exclusive stay.

For solo travellers and couples who prefer an intimate setting, we have a 3 room poolside Cabana with direct access to the pool deck lounge and jacuzzi.

Each of our areas have their own kitchens, living spaces and amenities, offering you the unique experience of high-class-living with our hospitable staff onhand to make your stay as comfortable as possible. We take pride in our service-forward approach, offering a very personal and accessible experience for all our guests.

For exclusive bookings, our whole compound can comfortably accommodate up to 25pax and can also host functions and events, like birthdays, wedding parties, reunions and company outings.
